Petroleum Storage Tank Facility

Fuel Storage • Safety Systems • Industrial Infrastructure

Project Overview

The Petroleum Storage Tank Facility project focuses on developing large-scale storage systems designed to safely hold crude oil, diesel, petrol, and other refined petroleum products. Engineered for long-term durability, these tanks ensure secure storage, efficient distribution, and compliance with international safety and environmental standards.

Technical Specifications

  • Storage Capacity: 10,000 - 150,000 cubic meters per tank
  • Tank Type: Floating roof, fixed roof, and double-wall tanks
  • Material Construction: Carbon steel with corrosion-resistant coating
  • Safety Systems: Fire suppression, leak detection, and pressure relief valves
  • Compliance: API 650 / API 620 tank design standards

Additional Information

The facility includes automated monitoring systems, vapor recovery units, spill containment structures, and enhanced grounding for lightning protection. These features ensure maximum safety, operational efficiency, and environmental protection, supporting stable energy supply chain management.
