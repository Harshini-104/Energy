Solar Panel Installation

What is included in Solar Panel Installation?

+

Professional installation includes site inspection, load calculation, panel mounting, wiring setup, and inverter configuration to ensure maximum energy efficiency.

What is Solar System Design?

+

Solar system design involves energy analysis, shading study, panel layout creation, inverter selection, and overall system optimization for best performance.

How does Solar Maintenance work?

+

Maintenance includes periodic cleaning, output checks, inverter inspections, and ensuring all connections are secure for smooth energy generation.

Do solar panels need regular repairs?

+

Repairs may be required for damaged panels, broken wiring, loose mounts, or inverter failures. Early detection helps avoid power loss.

How long does a solar installation take?

+

A standard residential installation usually takes 1-3 days, including mounting, electrical setup, and system testing.

What is the life span of solar panels?

+

Most solar panels last 25-30 years with proper maintenance, retaining around 80–90% efficiency over their lifetime.

Windmill Installation

What is included in a windmill installation?

+

Windmill installation includes site selection, soil testing, foundation building, tower erection, turbine mounting, and complete electrical integration.

How does a wind turbine generate electricity?

+

Wind rotates the turbine blades, converting kinetic energy into mechanical energy. This mechanical energy is converted into electrical power by the generator.

What maintenance is required for wind turbines?

+

Maintenance includes blade inspection, lubrication of moving parts, checking gearbox health, monitoring generator output, and tower safety checks.

How long does a windmill installation take?

+

Installation can take 3-7 days depending on tower height, weather conditions, site access, and foundation curing time.

What is the lifespan of a wind turbine?

+

Modern wind turbines last 20-25 years with regular servicing and proper monitoring of mechanical components.

Is wind energy suitable for all locations?

+

Wind energy works best in high-wind zones, open land, coastal areas, and elevated hills where wind flow is consistent and unobstructed.
